Data obtained from trade registries from the Stockholm International Peace Research Institute (SIPRI)

Rich Text Format ---script--> CSV

~24,000 entries, featuring the following data fields for transactions from 1950-2017:

  • buyers & sellers
  • order date
  • order quantity
  • weapons model
  • period of delivery
  • Trend Indicator Values (TIV)
    •

    Sources drawing from:

  • Newspapers and other periodicals
  • Monographs and annual reference works
  • Press releases, annual reports and other information published by arms producing companies
  • Defence budget documents and parliamentary records
  • Period of delivery
  • Blogs, discussion fora and other Internet publications
    •

    Dataset limitations:

  • Script likely did not pull all the data from their database
  • Changes in geopolitical boundaries over time
